Who is JellyCat?

Hi, there! My name is Amanda, but you can call me JellyCat.

If you're on my page, we've probably crossed paths somewhere — maybe the circus community, or a burn, or perhaps my previous life as a therapist. I'm a multi-faceted creature, and I've never been good at doing things halfway.

Here's what tends to surprise people: I'm actually pretty introverted. The communities I've found myself in just happen to be full of people who are deeply weird AND deeply kind, which makes showing up feel easy.

Right now I'm training lyra, going to as many concerts as I can, and learning to code. The thread connecting all of it? Still figuring that out.
