For a very long time, I lived a pretty sheltered life. In fact, I made it to the age of 36 without ever attending an actual concert...

But, once my partner took me to my first show in August 2025, I was hooked and started making up for lost time! Since I discovered EDM only recently, I'm still exploring the various sub-genres and figuring out what I like. Here are all the artists I've seen live so far and the ones I have plans to see soon.
